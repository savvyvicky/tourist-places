Top 10 Tourist Destinations

These destinations represent the best of India’s culture, heritage, nature and adventure. Each card includes a quick description so you can choose your next travel highlight.

Agra

Agra is a historic city in Uttar Pradesh, home to the Taj Mahal and outstanding Mughal monuments.

Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur features royal forts, palaces, bustling bazaars and rich Rajasthani culture.

Goa Beaches

Goa’s sandy beaches offer sunshine, nightlife, seafood and a relaxed coastal vibe along the Arabian Sea.

Varanasi

One of the world’s oldest living cities, Varanasi is a spiritual pilgrimage destination along the holy Ganges River.

Kerala Backwaters

Serene houseboat cruises through lush canals and palm-lined lagoons make Kerala one of India’s most peaceful escapes.

Ladakh

High-altitude deserts, Buddhist monasteries, and dramatic mountain scenery define this thrilling northern frontier.

Udaipur

The City of Lakes enchants with palace reflections, romantic boat rides and charming lakeside promenades.

Hampi

Ancient ruins set among giant boulders create a mystical archaeological landscape in Karnataka.

Andaman Islands

Crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and white-sand beaches make the Andamans a tropical island paradise.

Golden Temple

Amritsar’s Golden Temple shines as a peaceful spiritual site and a stunning example of Sikh architecture.
